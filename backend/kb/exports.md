# Exports

## What this covers
Exporting a completed questionnaire run as a formatted Excel report.

## Steps to export
1. Go to `/runs` and open a completed run.
2. Click **Export to Excel**.
3. The file downloads immediately to your browser.

## What the export includes
- All questions from the questionnaire
- AI-generated answers with confidence scores
- Source citations (document name + section)
- Gap flags where no evidence was found
- Summary sheet with run metadata

## Export limits
Your plan controls how many exports you can generate per month.
- **Starter**: limited exports per month
- **Growth**: more exports per month
- **Elite**: unlimited exports

Check your current usage at `/settings/billing`.

## Common errors
- **"Export limit reached"** — upgrade your plan at `/settings/billing`.
- **"Run not complete"** — only completed runs can be exported. Wait for the run to finish.

## Relevant pages
