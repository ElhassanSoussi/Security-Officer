# Plans & Billing

## What this covers
Understanding your subscription, usage limits, and how to manage billing.

## Plans overview
| Plan    | Documents | Projects | Runs/month | Price       |
|---------|-----------|----------|------------|-------------|
| Starter | 25        | 5        | 10         | $149/month  |
| Growth  | 100       | 20       | 25         | $499/month  |
| Elite   | Unlimited | Unlimited| Unlimited  | $1,499/month|

## Viewing your usage
1. Go to `/settings/billing`.
2. The Usage section shows documents, projects, and runs used vs. your limits.
3. Progress bars turn amber at 85% and red when at the limit.

## Upgrading your plan
1. Go to `/plans` and select the plan you want.
2. Click **Upgrade** — you will be redirected to Stripe checkout.
3. After payment, your plan updates immediately.

## Managing billing
1. Go to `/settings/billing`.
2. Click **Manage Billing** to open the Stripe customer portal.
3. From there you can update your payment method, view invoices, or cancel.

## Common questions
- **"Why am I blocked from uploading?"** — you may have hit your document or storage limit. Check `/settings/billing`.
- **"Why can't I start a run?"** — your monthly run limit may be reached. It resets on the first of the month.
- **"How do I cancel?"** — use the Manage Billing button to access the Stripe portal and cancel from there.
