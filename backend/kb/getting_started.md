# Getting Started

## What this covers

A quick guide to setting up your organization, uploading your first document, and running your first compliance questionnaire.

## Steps

1. **Create a project** — go to `/projects` and click "New Project". Give it a name (e.g. "SOC 2 Readiness 2026").
2. **Upload documents** — inside your project, click "Upload Document" and add your policies, control docs, or evidence files (PDF, DOCX, XLSX up to 10 MB).
3. **Run a questionnaire** — go to `/run`, select your project, attach a questionnaire file, and click "Start Run".
4. **Review results** — after the run completes, open the run detail page to review answers, confidence scores, and gaps.
5. **Export** — use the Export button to download an Excel report of the completed questionnaire.

## Common errors

- **"No documents found"** — upload at least one document to your project before running.
- **"File too large"** — maximum file size is 10 MB. Split large files before uploading.
- **"Storage limit reached"** — upgrade your plan at `/settings/billing`.
